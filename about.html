As our initiative grew, so did our ambitions. From organizing technical workshops to designing interdisciplinary projects like the F15 Fuego Buggy, our journey reflects our commitment to innovation and teamwork. We are proud to have evolved into a thriving community where students can explore their potential, learn hands-on skills, and contribute to meaningful advancements in technology and engineering.

Who we are

The F15 Fuego initiative is a vibrant community of students from diverse engineering backgrounds, including Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE), Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), and Computer Science Engineering (CSE) of government engineering college Idukki. Our members share a passion for problem-solving, innovation, and hands-on learning. We take pride in the collaborative spirit that drives every project, from brainstorming to implementation. Each member brings unique skills and perspectives to the table, making our projects both exciting and impactful.
