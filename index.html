Innovative Projects

Development of the F15 Fuego Buggy, an interdisciplinary engineering feat that combines mechanical, electronics, and software expertise. This project has become a symbol of innovation and teamwork within our college.

Creation of IoT-based remote control systems using Arduino and ESP32, which have been successfully integrated into real-world applications like vehicle tracking and home automation.

Technical Workshops

Will host hands-on workshops on IoT systems, LoRa integration, and embedded systems that drew significant participation from students across departments.

Community Engagement

Will Conduct technical talks in collaboration with IEEE and IEDC, inspiring students to explore cutting-edge technologies.
